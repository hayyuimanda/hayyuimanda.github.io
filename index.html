I'm Inda, an Indonesian Jardine Scholar at Exeter College, and I am interested in many facets of cybersecurity. This includes security protocols and mathematical cryptography, differential privacy, maritime cybersecurity, privacy-enhancing technology for conservation, as well as understanding cultural influences in privacy and security.
