Hayyu Imanda

DPhil Researcher in Cyber Security at the University of Oxford


I'm Inda, an Indonesian Jardine Scholar at Exeter College, and I am part of the CDT in Cyber Security programme at Oxford. I mostly study security protocols and applied cryptography, and look into adversary models that are outside the norm. 

I'm also interested in a wider span of topics in security & privacy; this includes post-quantum cryptography, differential privacy, and the mathematical foundations of cryptography.
